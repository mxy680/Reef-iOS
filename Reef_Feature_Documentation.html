1. Executive Summary

Reef is an innovative iPad application designed to transform the way college students study. By combining the familiar, intuitive annotation experience of apps like GoodNotes with cutting-edge AI assistance and engaging gamification mechanics, Reef creates a comprehensive learning ecosystem that adapts to each student's unique studying style.

Core Value Proposition

Reef eliminates the friction between studying and getting help. Instead of switching between a notes app, a search engine, and a tutor, students receive real-time, contextual AI feedback as they work through problems—during their natural pauses, without breaking their flow.

Key Differentiators

  • Proactive AI Assistance: Unlike chatbots that require explicit queries, Reef's AI observes your work and offers help at the right moment
  • Handwriting-Native: Full Apple Pencil support with intelligent handwriting recognition that understands mathematical notation
  • Gamified Mastery: Topic completion unlocks marine species that build your personal underwater reef, providing visual proof of progress
  • All-in-One Platform: Upload materials, annotate, practice, get feedback, and track progress—all without leaving the app

2. Target Audience & Market Position

Primary Audience: College Students

Reef is designed specifically for undergraduate and graduate students who need to master complex material efficiently. The app particularly excels for students in:

STEM Disciplines

Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Engineering, Computer Science—fields where problem-solving and mathematical notation are central to learning.

Pre-Professional Programs

Pre-med, Pre-law, and Business students who must absorb large volumes of material and demonstrate mastery through rigorous examinations.

Visual & Kinesthetic Learners

Students who learn best by writing, drawing, and actively engaging with material rather than passive reading or watching.

User Personas

The Struggling STEM Student

Maya is a sophomore taking Organic Chemistry. She understands concepts in lecture but gets stuck on practice problems at night when office hours are closed. She needs immediate, contextual help to work through problems step-by-step.

The Visual Organizer

James is a biology major who learns by creating detailed diagrams and annotating his textbook PDFs. He wants his notes, annotations, and practice problems in one searchable, organized place.

The Motivated Achiever

Priya is pre-med and highly motivated but needs external structure and rewards to maintain consistency. Gamification elements help her stay engaged through long study sessions.

4. Document Management System

Overview

Reef's document management system serves as the foundation for all study activities. It's designed to handle the diverse range of materials college students encounter while keeping everything organized and accessible.

Supported File Types

PDFs

Full support for multi-page PDFs including textbooks, lecture slides, research papers, and handouts. Maintains original formatting while enabling annotation overlay.

Images

JPEG, PNG, HEIC support for photographs of whiteboards, diagrams, handwritten notes, and any visual study material.

Future Formats

Planned support for PowerPoint/Keynote presentations, Word documents, and web page captures.

Organization Features

Course-Based Hierarchy

Documents are organized in a three-level hierarchy:

  1. Courses: Top-level folders (e.g., "CHEM 201 - Organic Chemistry")
  2. Units/Chapters: Subfolders within courses (e.g., "Unit 3: Stereochemistry")
  3. Documents: Individual files within units

Smart Tagging

  • Add custom tags to any document
  • AI-suggested tags based on content analysis
  • Filter and search across all documents by tag

Quick Access

  • Recent: Last 10 opened documents
  • Favorites: Starred documents for instant access
  • Due Soon: Documents linked to upcoming assignments or exams

Import Methods

  • Files App: Import from iCloud, Google Drive, Dropbox, or local storage
  • Camera Capture: Photograph physical documents with automatic edge detection and perspective correction
  • Share Extension: Import directly from other apps via iOS share sheet
  • Drag and Drop: Full Split View support for dragging files from other apps

6. AI-Powered Learning Assistant

The Revolutionary Feature

Reef's AI assistant represents a paradigm shift in how students get help while studying. Instead of requiring students to stop, formulate a question, and switch to a chatbot, Reef observes their work and provides assistance at natural breaking points.

How It Works

1
Continuous Observation

As you write with Apple Pencil, Reef monitors your work in real-time

↓
2
Pause Detection

Algorithms detect when you pause, indicating thought or confusion

↓
3
Work Clustering

AI determines which problem you're working on using spatial clustering

↓
4
Handwriting Transcription

Gemini 3 Pro converts your handwriting to LaTeX notation

↓
5
AI Analysis

LaTeX is sent to a reasoning model for understanding and feedback

↓
6
Contextual Feedback

Helpful guidance appears non-intrusively during your natural pause

Pause Detection Intelligence

The system uses multiple signals to detect meaningful pauses:

  • Duration: Pauses longer than a configurable threshold (default: 3 seconds)
  • Context: Distinguishing between "thinking" pauses and "reading" pauses
  • Pattern Recognition: Learning your personal pause patterns over time
  • Problem Boundaries: Detecting when you move to a new problem

Clustering Algorithms

When a document contains multiple problems, Reef uses spatial clustering to identify which work belongs to which problem:

  • Proximity-based grouping of strokes
  • Recognition of problem numbers and labels
  • Temporal sequencing (what was written when)
  • Content analysis (this equation relates to that problem)

Handwriting Recognition Pipeline

Powered by Gemini 3 Pro

Mathematical Notation

Accurately transcribes complex mathematical expressions including fractions, integrals, summations, matrices, Greek letters, and subscripts/superscripts.

Chemical Formulas

Recognizes molecular formulas, reaction arrows, structural diagrams, and standard chemistry notation.

Mixed Content

Handles documents with prose, equations, and diagrams interspersed together.

Customizable Reasoning Models

Users can select their preferred AI model for generating feedback:

Model Strengths Best For
GPT-4 Broad knowledge, strong reasoning General subjects, writing
Claude Nuanced explanations, safety Complex topics, ethics
Gemini Multimodal, diagram understanding Visual subjects, science
Custom/Local Privacy, specialized training Sensitive data, niche subjects

Feedback Types

Encouragement

When work is correct: brief positive confirmation to build confidence without interrupting flow.

Gentle Hints

When stuck: Socratic questions that guide toward the answer without giving it away.

Error Identification

When mistakes are detected: specific, constructive feedback about where reasoning went wrong.

Concept Connections

Links current work to related concepts, previous problems, or upcoming material.

Privacy & Control

  • On-Device Option: Keep handwriting processing local when possible
  • Selective Sharing: Choose which documents AI can access
  • Disable Anytime: Turn off AI features per-document or globally
  • Data Retention: Clear controls over what's stored and for how long

7. Quiz & Exam Generation Engine

Overview

Reef's quiz generation system transforms uploaded course materials into customized practice assessments. This active recall approach is proven to improve retention compared to passive review.

Generation Process

  1. Content Analysis: AI scans selected documents for key concepts, terms, and relationships
  2. Question Synthesis: Generates questions at the specified difficulty and format
  3. Answer Key Creation: Produces correct answers with explanations
  4. Review & Edit: Users can modify or remove questions before finalizing

Question Types

Multiple Choice

4-5 options with one correct answer. AI generates plausible distractors based on common misconceptions. Supports "select all that apply" variant.

Fill in the Blank

Key terms or values removed from statements. Can require exact match or accept synonyms. Supports multiple blanks per question.

Open-Ended / Free Response

Essay-style questions requiring written or drawn answers. AI grades based on rubric and key points. Supports mathematical work with LaTeX comparison.

Matching

Connect terms to definitions, concepts to examples, or causes to effects. Randomized presentation order.

Ordering

Arrange items in correct sequence (timeline, process steps, etc.).

Customization Options

Difficulty Levels

Level Description
Recall Direct factual questions testing memory
Understanding Explain concepts in own words, identify examples
Application Apply concepts to new scenarios
Analysis Break down complex problems, identify relationships
Synthesis Combine multiple concepts to solve novel problems

Exam Settings

  • Question Count: Set total number of questions (5-100+)
  • Time Limit: Optional countdown timer (5 minutes to 4 hours)
  • Question Distribution: Percentage per type or let AI optimize
  • Passing Score: Custom threshold for "passing" (default: 70%)
  • Randomization: Shuffle question order, shuffle answer choices
  • Retakes: Allow unlimited, limited, or no retakes

During the Exam

  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • Progress indicator showing questions remaining
  • Timer display (if timed)
  • Flag questions for review
  • Navigate between questions freely
  • Handwriting support for free-response questions

Results & Feedback

Immediate Feedback Mode

See if each answer is correct right after submitting. Great for learning.

Exam Mode

Results shown only after completing all questions. More realistic test prep.

Results Breakdown

  • Overall score and pass/fail status
  • Score by question type
  • Score by topic/concept
  • Time spent per question
  • Comparison to previous attempts
  • Detailed explanations for incorrect answers

Spaced Repetition Integration

Missed questions are automatically added to a review queue with optimized spacing:

  • Questions you got wrong appear sooner
  • Correctly answered questions space out over time
  • Daily review reminders based on optimal retention timing

8. Gamification: Species Unlocking System

Concept Overview

The Species Unlocking System transforms academic achievement into a visually rewarding collection game. As students master topics by passing exams, they unlock marine species that populate their personal reef—creating a living, growing representation of their knowledge.

Why Gamification Works

Research shows that gamification increases engagement, motivation, and retention in educational contexts. By making progress visible and collectible, students maintain momentum through difficult material and feel genuine accomplishment when mastering topics.

Topic-Species Mapping

Each academic topic is pre-mapped to a specific marine species. The mapping follows a meaningful pattern based on topic complexity:

Surface-Level Topics → Small, Common Species

Introductory concepts and foundational topics unlock smaller fish that inhabit shallow waters:

Clownfish

Basic definitions, introductory concepts

Blue Tang

Foundational principles

Seahorse

Simple processes, basic formulas

Angelfish

Core vocabulary, key terms

Mid-Depth Topics → Medium Species

Intermediate topics requiring synthesis of multiple concepts:

Sea Turtle

Multi-step processes

Dolphin

Applied problem-solving

Manta Ray

Interconnected concepts

Moray Eel

Complex mechanisms

Deep Topics → Large, Impressive Species

Advanced topics with multiple subtopics unlock apex predators and rare deep-sea creatures:

Great White Shark

Comprehensive mastery areas

Orca

Cross-disciplinary synthesis

Giant Squid

Theoretical frameworks

Whale Shark

Capstone achievements

Unlocking Mechanics

Requirements

  1. Complete an exam covering the topic
  2. Achieve the passing score (customizable, default 70%)
  3. Species unlock animation plays
  4. Species added to personal reef

Unlock Celebration

When a species is unlocked:

  • Full-screen animated reveal with species swimming into view
  • Species information card (name, scientific name, fun fact)
  • Connection to the academic topic explained
  • Sound effect and haptic feedback
  • Option to share achievement

Species Attributes

Each species has unique attributes that add depth to the collection:

Attribute Description
Rarity Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary
Habitat Zone Surface, Reef, Mid-water, Deep sea, Abyss
Animation Style Unique swimming pattern and behavior
Size Relative size in the reef display
Real-World Info Educational facts about the actual species

Special Unlocks

Achievement-Based Species

  • Perfect Exam: Score 100% to unlock golden variant
  • Speed Run: Complete under time limit for special coloring
  • Streak: 7-day study streak unlocks exclusive species
  • Course Complete: Finish all topics in a course for unique reward

Seasonal Species

  • Holiday-themed variants
  • Collaboration events with courses or professors

Data Models

Core Entities

  • User: Account info, preferences, settings
  • Course: Name, icon, color, associated topics
  • Document: File reference, pages, annotations
  • Annotation: Strokes, shapes, text boxes per page
  • Topic: Name, difficulty, associated species, prerequisites
  • Species: Name, rarity, animations, unlock conditions
  • Quiz: Questions, settings, user attempts
  • Attempt: Answers, score, duration, timestamp

Storage Architecture

Local Storage

  • Core Data: Structured data (users, courses, quizzes)
  • File System: Documents, images, exported PDFs
  • UserDefaults: Preferences, settings, feature flags

Cloud Sync

  • CloudKit: Cross-device sync for all user data
  • Automatic conflict resolution
  • Offline-first with background sync

Performance Considerations

  • Canvas Rendering: Metal-accelerated drawing at 120Hz on ProMotion displays
  • Large Documents: Tiled rendering for PDFs, only visible pages in memory
  • AI Latency: Background processing with optimistic UI updates
  • Animations: SpriteKit for efficient reef rendering at 60fps

12. Security & Privacy

Data Protection

At Rest

  • All local data encrypted using iOS Data Protection
  • Keychain storage for authentication tokens
  • CloudKit encryption for synced data

In Transit

  • TLS 1.3 for all network communications
  • Certificate pinning for API endpoints
  • No sensitive data in URLs or logs

AI Privacy

  • Minimal Data Transmission: Only the relevant work region sent for analysis
  • No Training on User Data: User content not used to train AI models
  • Configurable AI Access: Users control which documents AI can process
  • Data Retention Controls: Choose how long AI interaction history is kept
  • Audit Log: View exactly what was sent to AI services

Authentication Security

  • Secure password requirements (12+ characters, complexity rules)
  • Sign in with Apple support (privacy-preserving)
  • Biometric unlock (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Session management with automatic expiration
  • Rate limiting on login attempts

Compliance

  • FERPA: Ready for educational institution deployment
  • COPPA: Age verification for users under 13
  • GDPR: Full data export and deletion capabilities
  • App Store Guidelines: Full compliance with Apple requirements
